## Post 2
**Likes:** 19,200
**Content:**
Most people suck at content creation because they think they need to be original.
You don’t need to be original. You need to be consistent and clear.

How I generated 500 million impressions over the last 4 years:
↳ I write down the top 10 problems my target audience faces.
↳ I write 10 different ways to solve each problem.
↳ I spend 60 minutes every morning turning those into structured posts.

My most successful format:
1. The Hook: Call out the specific pain point.
2. The Story: How I experienced and overcame it.
3. The Framework: Step-by-step instructions.
4. The Takeaway: A one-sentence summary.

Stop trying to be Hemingway. Start being helpful.
