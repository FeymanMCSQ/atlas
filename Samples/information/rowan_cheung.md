# Rowan Cheung
**Platform:** X / LinkedIn
**Style:** Breaking News, Speed, Actionable Utility
**Vibe:** Expressive, urgent, visually-driven.

## Key Principles:
1. **The Hook:** Starts immediately with a siren/alert emoji and a massive claim ("AI just changed X forever").
2. **The "Meat":** Bullet points summarizing heavy research papers or multi-page announcements in 10 words or less per bullet.
3. **The Utility:** Never just says "this exists". Constantly explains "how you can use this today to work 10x faster."
4. **The Visual:** Almost always accompanied by a GIF, architecture diagram, or screen recording.
5. **The Structure:** Uses emojis as bullet points. Keeps paragraphs to 1-2 lines.

## Example Post:
🚨 Google just released Gemini 1.5 Pro and it changes everything.

It has a massive 1 Million Token context window. You can drop an entire 1-hour video or 30,000 lines of code into it.

Here is why this matters:

1️⃣ RAG is taking a huge hit. You no longer need complex databases to search code.
2️⃣ Video Analysis is automated. Drop an MP4 in, get timestamped analysis instantly.
3️⃣ Code refactoring is solved. Upload your entire codebase and say "rewrite this in Rust."

The bar for AI applications was just raised. If your startup was just a PDF wrapper, you're in trouble.
