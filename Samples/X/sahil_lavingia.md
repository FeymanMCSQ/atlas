I wanted to build a billion dollar company.
I raised $8M from Tier 1 VCs. I hired 20 people. We burned through the cash, and I failed. I had to lay off my entire team.

**Content:**
The simplest business model in the world:
1. Learn a valuable skill.
2. Use that skill to build a product or solve a problem.
3. Teach others how you did it for free.
4. Sell the templates, software, or advanced courses to those who want to do it faster.

You don't need a massive team. You just need to be 10% ahead of the people you are teaching.
